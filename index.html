Key Features

  • Hero Roster Variety: Play as a swordmaster, shadow rogue, magic archer, or kung-fu monk — each with distinct combat roles.

  • Roguelike Combat Engine: Every run is different with randomized dungeons and unpredictable enemy formations.

  • Chibi Anime Aesthetic: Beautifully animated 2D heroes with exaggerated emotions and smooth movement.

  • Elemental Powers: Upgrade your skills and unlock fire, ice, thunder, and void powers through the Dreamy Wizard.

  • Easy but Punchy Controls: Tap-to-slash mechanics and directional dodge let you fight fluidly.

  • Permadeath + Progression: Each run helps you unlock better gear and meta-boosts for stronger revivals.

  • Enemy Diversity: From sneaky goblins to colossal knights, every encounter requires adaptation.
